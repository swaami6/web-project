COVID-19 Testing Services

Individual Testing

If you are experiencing symptoms of COVID-19 or have been in close contact with someone who has tested positive, we offer COVID-19 testing services for individuals. Our goal is to help prevent the spread of COVID-19 by providing fast and accurate testing results.

  • PCR Testing
  • Rapid Antigen Testing
  • Antibody Testing

Organizational Testing

If you are an organization looking to test your employees or members, we offer on-site COVID-19 testing services. Our trained professionals will come to your location to administer the tests and provide fast and accurate results.

  • PCR Testing
  • Rapid Antigen Testing
  • Antibody Testing
